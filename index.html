Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before your visit.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, walk-ins are accepted based on availability. For faster service and minimal waiting, we recommend booking ahead.

How often should I get my teeth cleaned?

Most patients benefit from professional teeth cleaning every 6 months. We may suggest a personalized schedule depending on oral health needs.

Are dental treatments painful?

We use pain-conscious methods and comfort-focused care to keep treatment as gentle as possible. Your comfort is continuously monitored.

Do you treat children?

Absolutely. Our pediatric dental care is family-friendly, gentle, and designed to make children feel safe and relaxed.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book instantly by phone, WhatsApp, or the online form below. Our team will contact you shortly to confirm your visit.

What should I do in a dental emergency?

Call us immediately for guidance. For severe pain, swelling, bleeding, or injury, we prioritize urgent evaluation as quickly as possible.

Do you offer cosmetic dentistry?

Yes. We provide teeth whitening, veneers, aligners, and smile makeovers with personalized planning for natural-looking outcomes.

Do you accept insurance?

We provide insurance assistance and can help you understand available options. Final coverage depends on your specific provider and plan.
